What it solves..

With the meteoric rise of the IT industry and its democratization, it remains one of the most lucrative job destinations across the board. With the multitude of aspiring candidates, who are aiming to kickstart their IT careers every year, it has become essential for a smart and efficient recruitment process that can ensure fair evaluation criteria. In this project, we have created a Voice Bot, capable of taking campus interviews in English. The Bot has been built using RASA open-source framework (RASA 3.x), and it is capable of conducting rapid-fire interviews on common subjects of Computer Science, both textually and by voice. The bot functions by asking 20 MCQ questions on relevant subjects and grades the candidates based on their responses. The grade is then displayed after the interview round gets over. The grade evaluated by the bot can be used to screen potential employees from the candidate population for further recruitment processes. We hope that the bot will be valuable for Hiring Managers and also for candidates who can take interviews at their ease.
